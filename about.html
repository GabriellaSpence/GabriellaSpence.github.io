About Me

I am a people analytics professional passionate about driving decisions with data and developing automation solutions. I have a background in Industrial-Organizational Psychology and found my home in the comfort of data and coding. I began this website to document my professional development journey in June 2022. Hopefully, as time goes by, you will find something interesting in the pages of my blog or the personal projects I present.

My Experience

Evolent Health: Serve on a two-member Talent Analytics team as an analyst aligned to all sub-departments in the HR team (e.g., recruiting, L&D, HR Ops, etc.). My role is to help build the foundation of our data and reporting systems. My work includes survey development and analysis, data validations and preprocessing, report automation, and dashboarding. Although my core focus is on operational and prescriptive reporting, occasionally, there is time for more advanced analytics projects. As I progress further in my career, my goals are to gain more knowledge and exposure to become experienced with advanced and predictive analytics.
