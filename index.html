Evolent Health: Serve on a two-member Talent Analytics team as an analyst aligned to all sub-departments in the HR team (e.g., recruiting, L&D, HR Ops, etc.). My role is to help build the foundation of our data and reporting systems. My work includes survey development and analysis, data validations and preprocessing, report automation, and dashboarding. Although my core focus is on operational and prescriptive reporting, occasionally, there is time for more advanced analytics projects. As I progress further in my career, my goals are to gain more knowledge and exposure to become experienced with advanced and predictive analytics. Below are some example projects I completed end-to-end:

  • Create a resignation dashboard in R Shiny to help HR Ops track a new self-service termination tool.
  • Create a turnover cost model for the CSR population to help determine opportunities to reduce costs and estimate theoretical spending on turnover in a given year.
  • Generate personalized PDF reports to send to leaders with key metrics regarding their span of control.
  • Design, produce and distribute scorecards for managers to understand their performance based on Engagement Survey results.
  • Create an internal R package that enables team members to run common data preprocessing steps and calculate metrics for quick turnaround.
  • Enable email automation across the team.
